Sacred Bowl Orbit

Chrysasynth — Standalone Edition

Thank you for downloading this tool.

Sacred Bowl Orbit is a meditative resonance instrument
from the Chrysasynth lab.

It generates harmonic singing bowl tones
that evolve through orbital motion and spatial resonance.

HOW TO USE

Extract the folder

Open "index.html" in your browser

Click "Resonate" to activate audio

Drag the resonance point around the bowl

Explore different bowls and modulation controls

Enable Orbit mode for continuous harmonic motion.

CONTROLS

Resonate — starts the bowl resonance
Orbit — enables automatic circular motion
Stop — stops the sound

Intensity — controls the energy of the bowl
Space — controls reverb and spatial depth
Orbit Speed — controls the motion of the resonance point

BOWLS

Root — grounding resonance
Sacral — warm flowing tone
Solar — radiant harmonic clarity
Heart — open balanced resonance
Throat — bright harmonic articulation
Third Eye — expansive overtone field
Crown — ethereal celestial resonance

Each bowl has its own harmonic structure
and spectral response.
